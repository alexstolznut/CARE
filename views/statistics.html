You are not alone in the fight against Mental Illness


Many veterans and people in service have a mental illness. These illnesses can be treated at a clinic, hospital, or VA treatment center, either through inpatient or outpatient treatment. We at Americare want to encourage veterans and service users (VA service users) to seek out treatment. The number of veterans and service users that visit our veteran stations have a high percentage of possible or confirmed mental illness. We want them to get diagnosed and get the treatment they need to live a better life.


Proportion of Veterans with Possible Mental Illness


Many veterans come into veteran stations around the United States not even knowing that they may possibly have a mental illness. 69% of veterans coming into these stations and are seen by workers there may possibly have a mental health illness.

Proportion of Veterans with Confirmed Mental Illness


85% of veterans that come into our stations are confirmed to have some mental illness. If you suspect that you may have something, we urge you to come in and seek a diagnosis and maybe even a treatment. Many veterans have mental healh issues, you are not alone!

Percent of Service Users with Possible Mental Illness


If you are not a veteran and still in service, you may still have a mental illness. 31% of service users that come to veteran stations are suspected of a possible mental illness. It is not just veterans that have this problem, but people still in service do too, although less. This does not mean that you should not seek help if you suspect something.

Proportion of Veterans with Confirmed Mental Illness in Inpatient Mental Health Treatment - Let's Raise that Number!


This is the proportion of veterans with a confirmed mental illness that are getting in-patient treatment. At only 4%, we at Americare want to help raise that number up. There are 85% of veterans coming into these stations with a confirmed mental illness. If you knoe you have a mental illness, it is okay to go and seek treatment. Let's increase this number and fight mental illness together!

Proportion of Service Users Who Received Mental Health Services


Only 25% of service users coming into our veteran stations receive mental health services. If you suspect anything, you can get diagnosised and treated too.

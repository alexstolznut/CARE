Our Goal

Our team at Americare wants to inform veterans and service users about mental illness in a way that will encourage them to ask questions and to seek help if they need it. Americare welcomes all users, but wants those who are currently suffering from a mental illness and want support to learn more and to find the resources they need to get better. Veterans and Service Users who just want to support their fellow veterans as well can use the site to share their experiences and stories too (and even doctors if they wish to participate). Americare is a safe platform for anyone who has ever felt confused or alone while dealing with their mental illness. We want them to feel safe and to feel that they are not alone in their struggle.

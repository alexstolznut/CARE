Our Goal

We at Americare wants to inform users about mental illness, in a way that will encourage them to ask questions. Americare welcomes all users, but tries to target those who currently are suffering from a mental illness and want support, people who just want to support those struggling with a mental illness, and even doctors if they wish to participate. Americare is a safe platform for anyone who has ever felt alone.
